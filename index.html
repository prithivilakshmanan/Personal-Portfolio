Project Source

Roof Top Irrigation System

Abstraction:

  • In my academics, I completed a project named "Roof Top Irrigation System" based on IoT.
  • The project's goal is to address water scarcity by efficiently irrigating water.
  • In urban areas, where space is limited, people can meet their gardening needs through roof gardening.
  • The height of the roof varies depending on the gardening plans.
  • The project introduces various irrigation methods such as springular irrigation, drip irrigation, and a rainfall irrigation system.
  • Components used in the project include a soil moisture sensor to analyze water levels, an Arduino board for hardware control, and a stepper motor for even water distribution.

Working Principle:

If the soil lacks sufficient water, the soil sensor sends a message to the Arduino board, triggering the stepper motor to irrigate the water clockwise and return to its original position.
